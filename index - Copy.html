My product

Protecting biodiversity while powering the future.

X-Band Radar

Short-range detection that identifies incoming birds and bats with precision, even in complex environments.

360° Scanning

Our rotating radar antenna provides continuous surveillance across the entire wind farm perimeter.

AI Computer Vision

Advanced AI cameras track flight paths in real-time, distinguishing between species to trigger safety protocols.

Acoustic Sensors

Specialized microphones detect bat echolocation and bird calls during low visibility or night operations.

Signal Processing Unit

Analyzes radar and camera data, filters noise, tracks objects.

AI Collision Prediction Software

Predicts bird flight paths and calculates collision risk.

Migration Prediction Module

Uses historical data to forecast large flocks.

Ultrasonic Speakers & LED Lights

- Emit high-frequency sounds to scare birds away. - Flashing lights warn birds of turbines.

Our Mission

At SmartSky Energy, we believe renewable energy shouldn't come at the cost of local wildlife. Our integrated sensing suite ensures that wind turbines c an operate at peak efficiency while automatically slowing or stopping when protected species are in the flight path.
